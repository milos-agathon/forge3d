# P6 — Atmospherics & sky (Hosek–Wilkie/Preetham, volumetric fog/light shafts)

**Goal:** Physical sky and single-scattering fog.

**Backend/WGSL**

* `sky.wgsl` implementing Hosek–Wilkie & Preetham; sun direction from directional light (tagged as “sun”).
* Volumetrics: exponential height fog with Henyey–Greenstein phase; froxelized or view-ray single-scatter (compute).
* God-rays (volumetric shadows) sampled from shadow map; jittered integration with temporal reprojection.

**CLI**

* `--sky hosek-wilkie --turbidity 2.5 --ground-albedo 0.2`
* `--volumetric 'density=0.015,phase=hg,g=0.7,max_steps=48'`

**Acceptance**

* Sunrise/sunset color shift; beams visible when sun occluded by terrain; stable temporally.
